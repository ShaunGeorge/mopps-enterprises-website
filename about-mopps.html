Our Story

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Excellence

Mopp's Enterprises (PTY) LTD was established in 2002 by Chantelle Mopp with a mission to raise the standards of quality and precision in the South African automotive sector. What began as a small, family-led operation has grown into a reliable partner for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), Tier 1 & Tier 2 suppliers, and logistics partners.

We hold the proud distinction of being the first rework company to partner with Mercedes-Benz and remain the only Black-woman-owned quality control company in South Africa. With over 21 years of hands-on experience, we've established ourselves as a trusted name in automotive sorting, rework, and quality assurance services.
