What We Do

Recover Components.
Protect Your Output.

We rework parts that fall short of specifications due to cosmetic, labeling, or minor functional issues — helping clients recover valuable components quickly and reliably, minimising scrap and reducing cost.

Our skilled technicians have decades of combined experience across bodyshop, assembly, and component rework environments, making us uniquely equipped to handle complex rework challenges for the automotive sector.

Our Rework Capabilities
  • Correcting misassembled or incorrectly assembled parts
  • Label updates, documentation correction and re-labeling
  • Component cleaning, restoration and surface treatment
  • Functional testing, adjustments and re-verification
  • Sub-assembly and assembly rework operations
  • Full documentation and traceability throughout
How It Works

Our Rework Process

01

Assessment

Non-conforming components are assessed and a rework plan is developed, detailing the required corrective actions and expected outcomes.

02

Rework Execution

Skilled technicians carry out the rework according to OEM specifications, using approved tooling, materials, and methods.

03

Re-Inspection

Every reworked component is re-inspected to confirm it now meets the required quality standard before it is cleared for use.

04

Documentation & Return

Full rework records are produced and parts are re-packaged and returned or delivered to your line, ready for production.
