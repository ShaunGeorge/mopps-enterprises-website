Mopp's Enterprises (PTY) LTD, established in 2002 by Chantelle Mopp, stands as a pioneering quality control and rework company based in East London. We take pride in our distinguished history as the first rework company to partner with Mercedes-Benz. Notably, we are the only Black-woman-owned quality control company in South Africa and proudly hold a Level 1 B-BBEE certification.
