Precision Sorting
What We Do

Stop Defects Before
They Reach the Line

Our sorting services ensure your automotive parts meet strict quality standards before they enter production. We identify, isolate, and document defective parts — protecting your line from costly interruptions and quality escapes.

With over 21 years of experience serving OEMs and Tier 1 & 2 suppliers, our sorting teams are trained to handle high-volume, time-sensitive sorting operations with speed and precision.

What's Included
  • Initial intake quality check and batch logging
  • Visual and dimensional defect identification
  • Damage, cosmetic and functional sorting
  • Separation and labeling of good vs. rejected parts
  • Detailed reporting and defect trend analysis
  • Custom-tailored sorting criteria per client spec
How It Works

Our Sorting Process

01

Intake Inspection

All incoming parts are received, counted, and logged into our traceability system with a unique batch reference before sorting begins.

02

Sorting & Identification

Each component is individually inspected against agreed criteria — defective, damaged, or non-conforming parts are isolated and set aside.

03

Reporting

Results are documented in a clear defect report, including quantities, defect types, images where applicable, and trend data.

04

Packaging & Dispatch

Conforming parts are re-packaged and labeled for return to your line. Rejected parts are handled per your agreed disposition instructions.
