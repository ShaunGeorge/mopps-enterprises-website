Our Story

Pouring Back into the
Soil That Nurtured Us

After 23 years of growth, resilience and community support, Mopp's Enterprises is launching an initiative dedicated to giving back to the very community that raised us.

Introducing — Mopps Heart ❤️ a non-profit charity drive rooted in love, gratitude and purpose. This initiative was created to support and uplift the people of KuGompo, the very community where our owner, Chantelle Mopp, grew up and was shaped into the business woman she is today.

As we begin this journey, our vision is not only to serve KuGompo, but to expand our reach to Qonce — formerly known as King William's Town — the hometown of Mark Mopp, the founder who started Mopp's Enterprises with a dream of building something bigger than himself.

Together, we rise by lifting others.

Introducing Mopps Heart ❤️ — We're Officially Launching

After 23 years of building, growing, and serving the automotive industry, we felt a pull back to our roots. The people of KuGompo gave our founder, Chantelle Mopp, the foundation she needed to become who she is today. And now, Mopp's Enterprises is officially giving back.

Mopps Heart is our non-profit charity drive — a permanent commitment to the communities of KuGompo and Qonce. This isn't a once-off event. This is a movement that will grow with every drive we run and every life we touch.

"We believe in pouring back into the soil that nurtured us."

Our inaugural announcement marks the beginning of a journey focused on tangible community support — from food and clothing to education and mentorship. Watch this space as our story unfolds.
